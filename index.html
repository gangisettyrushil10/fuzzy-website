01

An AI tutor inside every document.

Select any passage and ask Fuzzy to explain, simplify, or quiz you on it. Your tutor reads the same document you do — it has full context, not just the sentence you highlighted.

Explain Simplify Quiz me Summarise
02

Beautiful reading for every format.

PDF, EPUB, Word, Markdown — Fuzzy imports them all into one library. Books render with proper typography and reading themes. PDFs keep their layout. Everything is searchable.

PDF EPUB DOCX Markdown TXT
03

A library that knows your books.

Every import lands in a unified library with chapter-level navigation. Expand any book to jump straight to a chapter — no more hunting for where you left off.

Chapter navigation Resume position Annotations
04

Turn any book into a study pack.

Generate AI-powered flashcards and quizzes from any document in one click. Spaced repetition tracks which cards you know. Review on your schedule.

Flashcards Quizzes Spaced repetition Key concepts

Read faster. Drift less.

Focus mode strips the UI down to just the page. The WPM pacer sweeps word by word at your chosen speed, training your eyes to stop skipping lines.
